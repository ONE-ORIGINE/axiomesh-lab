# Evolution Notes

This repository captures the pre-product and sandbox lineage behind AxiomMesh.

## Major lines present here

1. **EDP v4.1**
   - mathematical environment modeling
   - circumstances, harmony, causal gravity, phenomenon detection

2. **MEP iterations**
   - protocol evolution from structured envelopes to more agent-oriented layers
   - provider experiments with Ollama, OpenAI, Anthropic
   - explainability, retries, circuit breakers, semantic intent translation

3. **SAVOIR**
   - known vs estimated distinction
   - certainty tensor / environmental state matrix
   - reaction transition modeling

4. **Drone-first direction**
   - drone-specific semantic adaptation
   - swarm reasoning
   - contextual action gating for navigation, flight, emergency, scan

5. **Satellite components**
   - contextualizer
   - impact analytics
   - examples and protocol fragments

## Why it matters

AxiomMesh SDK is the stabilized public form.
This lab repository preserves the exploratory DNA.
